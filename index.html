What Is Auto Detailing?

This professional service goes far beyond a quick rinse at a drive-through wash. It delivers deep cleaning, restoration, and protection for both the interior and exterior of your vehicle.

Auto Detailing Overview – Is a thorough cleaning and reconditioning process for both the interior and exterior of a vehicle. It goes far beyond a standard car wash, focusing on restoring your car’s original look and feel.

What’s Included in Detailing – Detailing services often include hand washing, paint decontamination, polishing, and interior shampooing. Professional detailers also focus on small details cleaning vents, restoring trim, conditioning leather, and eliminating odors.

FAQs 

Q1. Is it safe to detail a brand-new car?

Absolutely. In fact, detailing a new car can help protect it from the start and remove factory residues or imperfections.

Q2. Can I drive my car immediately after detailing?

Yes, but if protective coatings or waxes were applied, it’s best to avoid rain or washing for 24 hours to allow them to cure.

Q3. How often should I get my car detailed?

Most people benefit from detailing every three to six months, depending on driving habits and exposure to harmful elements.
